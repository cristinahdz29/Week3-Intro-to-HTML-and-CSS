My Children (aka pets)

I am a proud parent of 2 insanely adorable and lovable cats named Peluza and Kylo.

Peluza

Peluza came into my life about 7 years ago. Her mom had a litter of kittens under our shed and Peluza was the only one who stauyed behind. Her name literally translates to "fluffy" in Spanish (I know, so original lol). She is a very unique cat in that she hates everything that a typical cat would love including catnip, water fountains, scratching posts, toys, and wet food. However, she LOVES cuddling, so I guess that makes up for it! Sadly, I had to leave her back home with my parents after moving to Atlanta because she is an older indoor/outdoor cat who hates new environments. But I often send care packages home to her!!

Kylo

Kylo is my husband's cat that I have come to love as my own!!! He is the total opposite of Peluza. In fact, he is the total opposite of a cat and acts more like a dog. He is obsessed with temptations treats, loves to play with his toys and is very sociable. He also plays fetch with his toy mice.
